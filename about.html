ABOUT

Kyurious Minds is an online Student Support Forum , which aims at creating a platform for students and their teachers to interact and discuss about different academic problems. We aim to bridge the student-teacher communication gap through this platform and try to make education better in India.

VISION

To provide a huge database of readable and informative topics for every child in India promoting free education. Thus providing every child an opportunity for educating himself
